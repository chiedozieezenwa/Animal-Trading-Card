Bengal Tiger

Bengal tigers are the national animal of both Bangladesh and India.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Length: 6.8ft - 11ft
  • Average lifespan: 18-25 years
  • Habitat: Dense tropical forest and mangroves
  • Weight: 140kg - 300kg
  • Predators: Human
  • Diet: Carnivore
